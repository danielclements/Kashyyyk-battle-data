The Battle of Kashyyyk

The Battle of Kashyyyk, also known as the Battle of Kachirho, was a prolonged campaign that took place during the Outer Rim Sieges and was among the final conflicts of the Clone Wars. It was fought on the planet Kashyyyk,the homeworld of the Wookiees and a vital Republic member world. The Mytaranor sector world had been invaded by the Confederacy of Independent Systems hoping to claim the Kashyyyk hyperspace junction, as well as gain information on secret hyperspace routes rumored to be in Wookiee archives, that could turn the war in its favor.
